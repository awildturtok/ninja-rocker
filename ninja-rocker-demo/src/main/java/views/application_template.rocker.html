Application-specific template


Rocker templates are subclasses of a parent template that adds/modifies behavior. When you access a variable or method in your template, its not magic where its coming from -- that variable or method MUST exist in the hierarchy of classes your template derives from. By controlling what parent your templates extends, you can add other variables or methods that can be elegantly accessed in your template. The template your viewing has a class hierarchy of:
com.fizzed.rocker.runtime.DefaultRockerTemplate -> com.fizzed.ninja.rocker.NinjaRockerTemplate -> utils.ApplicationRockerTemplate -> views.application_template

In the following snippets below, the utils.ApplicationRockerTemplate class is what adds the A variable to expose a couple extra properties.
